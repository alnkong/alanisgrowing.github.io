2021-01-20 2 min read

Questions for Unit 6, 8, 9, and 10

The following questions are formulated by Alan based on the titles of each unit and the intercultural competence column of the map of the book.

Unit 6 Gender

  1. Do you think there are genders other than men and women?
  2. What are some of the genders other than men and women?
  3. What do you self-identify as?
  4. Are you happy being a man/woman/other? Why?
  5. Do you think men and women are actually equal in our society? How about in other parts of the world? How are they equal/unequal?
  6. What are some of the questions you'd like to get answered regarding this topic?

Unit 8 News and Media

  1. How do you stay tuned to what's happening in the world around you?
  2. What do you think of the content available on our media?
  3. How does media content shape us and our society?
  4. What are some of the questions you'd like to get answered regarding this topic?

Unit 9 The Internet

  1. What do you use the Internet for?
  2. Do you consider yourself good at making the most of the Internet to improve your life and studies?
  3. Are you aware of any popular language that originiated from the Internet? Which one do you like best?
  4. What are some of the questions you'd like to get answered regarding this topic?

Unit 10 Cyber Security

  1. Have you experienced any cyber security incidents? What happened?
  2. What are some of the questions you'd like to get answered regarding this topic?
